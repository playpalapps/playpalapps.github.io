Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new time zone?

Tap the "+ Add" button in the top right corner of the World Clock screen. Search for any city or timezone and tap to add it.

How do I remove a time zone?

Swipe left on any timezone card to remove it. You'll see an undo option in case you remove one by accident.

How do I reorder my time zones?

Long-press and drag any timezone card to reorder it in the list.

What does the time slider do?

The slider lets you scrub through the day to see what time it will be in all your zones at any point. Tap "Now" to snap back to the current time.

How does "Find a Time" work?

Select 2 or more zones, and the app calculates the best meeting times where the most people are within work hours (9 AM - 5 PM by default). You can customise your work hours on the Availability tab.

Can I change my work hours?

Yes! Go to the Availability tab and tap the Start or End time to adjust your working hours.

Why do I see ads?

Time Zone Connect is free to use. Ads help support ongoing development. We use Google AdMob and do not collect personal data ourselves.

Is it available on both iOS and Android?

Yes! Time Zone Connect is available on the App Store for iOS and Google Play for Android.
