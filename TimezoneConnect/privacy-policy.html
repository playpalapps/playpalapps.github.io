Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Time Zone Connect does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All timezone calculations happen locally on your device. We use Google AdMob to display ads, which may collect limited data as described below.

Information We Collect

Time Zone Connect itself does not collect any personal information. All data you enter into the app (time zones, work hours, conversions) is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to our servers or any third party.

Locally Stored Data

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Selected time zones: The cities/zones you add to your world clock
  • Work hours: Your configured start and end work times
  • Theme preference: Your light/dark mode choice
  • Reminders: Any timezone reminders you set

This data never leaves your device and is cleared when you uninstall the app.

Advertising

Time Zone Connect uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data and interaction with ads
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS, the app requests your permission before allowing ad networks to track your activity across other apps and websites. You can change this at any time:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > disable tracking for Time Zone Connect

If you decline tracking, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized to your interests.

Ad Personalization

Google may use the data collected through AdMob to show personalized ads. You can opt out:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
Consent & GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we comply with GDPR and related data protection laws. Upon first use, you may be shown a consent dialog that allows you to consent, manage options, or decline personalized data processing. If you decline, you will still see non-personalized ads.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, object to processing, and withdraw consent at any time.

Since Time Zone Connect does not collect personal data directly, these rights primarily apply to data collected by Google AdMob. You can exercise them through Google's Privacy Controls or by contacting us.

Third-Party Services
  • Google AdMob: For serving advertisements — Privacy Policy
Children's Privacy

Time Zone Connect is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

Since Time Zone Connect does not collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal security risk. All locally stored preferences are protected by your device's own security measures.

Data Retention

Time Zone Connect does not retain any personal data on its servers. Locally stored preferences persist until you clear the app data or uninstall the app. For data retained by Google AdMob, refer to Google's Data Retention Policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
