How it plays

Tap. Defend. Survive.

1

Tap to fire

Tap anywhere on screen — Pip fires a beam from the Dynamic Island toward that point. Hit chains build a multiplier.

2

Tap Pippet

Tap the Dynamic Island itself to unleash a screen-clearing shockwave. Limited charges. Use them well.

3

Defend the notch

Asteroids, bombs, splitters and bosses rise from below. Don't let them reach Pip. Score, combo, ascend the leaderboard.
