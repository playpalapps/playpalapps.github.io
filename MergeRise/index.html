A merge puzzle is a 30-second loop. The systems around it are where weeks turn into months. Here's what keeps the second run from ever being the same as the first.

Daily Challenges

A new seed, a new modifier, same 24 hours for everyone. Chase the leaderboard or just chase yesterday's score.

Zen Mode

No timer, no modifier, no noise. Just the board and the next number. Pour a coffee, merge for an hour.

The Codex

Every tile you merge is logged with hand-written lore. Four skins × every tier = a small museum of your progress.

Achievements

Eight real milestones — Village Elder to Castle Lord. No checkbox busywork; each one marks a genuine leap in skill.

Missions

Rotating goals that reward how you play, not just how long. Chain-driven, merge-driven, or pure endurance — pick your lane.

Built for the commute

30-second runs for the train, hour-long sessions for the couch. Resumes instantly, saves every tile, never begs for your data.
