Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Merge Rise handles your information.

Effective Date: April 20, 2026

Introduction

PlayPal Apps ("we", "our", or "us") built Merge Rise as a free, ad-supported puzzle game. This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Merge Rise itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game data — including best scores, unlocked achievements, daily streak progress, XP, active skin, codex entries, missions, and settings — is stored locally on your device using the operating system's standard preference storage and is never sent to our servers.

We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, or any other personal details to play the game.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob — We use AdMob to display advertisements, including banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded video ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data to serve personalized or contextual ads. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Apple In-App Purchase / Google Play Billing — Used to process the one-time "Remove Ads" purchase. Transactions are handled entirely by Apple or Google; we never see your payment information. See Apple's Privacy Policy or Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — The platform through which you download the app may collect data as outlined in their respective privacy policies.

Ad Personalization

You may opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

If you purchase "Remove Ads," the ads SDK is not initialized at all on subsequent launches.

Children's Privacy

Merge Rise is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through a third-party service, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Security

Because we do not collect or transmit personal data ourselves, the security risk from our end is minimal. All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device using the operating system's standard secure local storage.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
