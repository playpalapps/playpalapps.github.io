Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save a tool to favorites?

Long-press any tool card on the home screen, or tap the bookmark icon inside any tool.

How do I switch between light and dark mode?

Go to Settings and toggle the Dark Mode switch.

Are my calculations stored anywhere?

No. All calculations happen locally on your device and nothing is sent to any server. The only tools that touch the network are Currency, Crypto Prices, and IP Info — and even those only request public data, never anything you type.

How do I change the app language?

Open Settings, tap Language, and pick one of 19 languages — English, Spanish, Hindi, Portuguese, French, German, Japanese, Indonesian, Italian, Chinese, Arabic, Korean, Russian, Turkish, Vietnamese, Thai, Polish, Bengali, or Tamil. Translations are machine-assisted, so let us know if anything reads oddly.

Why do I see ads?

Utilify is free. A small banner ad on each tool helps us keep it that way. We use Google AdMob and never see your personal data ourselves.

How do I change my ad-consent choice?

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland you saw a consent dialog on first launch. iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > Utilify to opt out of personalized ads. Android: Settings > Google > Ads. You'll still see ads, just non-personalized ones.

Does Utilify work offline?

Yes — almost all 45 tools run entirely on your device. Only Currency, Crypto Prices, and IP Info need a network connection.

Is the decibel meter accurate?

It is approximate. The microphone in a phone is not a calibrated sound-level meter, so values are an estimate — useful for "is this room loud?" but not for safety or medical decisions.

Is Utilify available on both iOS and Android?

Yes — Utilify is on the App Store for iOS and Google Play for Android.
