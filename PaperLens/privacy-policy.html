Privacy Policy

Version 1.0 · Effective April 2026

🔒 The short version: PaperLens processes everything on your device. We collect no personal data, send nothing to servers, and require no account. Your documents are yours, always.

Who We Are

PaperLens is developed and published by PlayPal Apps, an independent developer. By using this app you agree to the terms described in this policy.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. PaperLens does not require an account, does not track you, and does not send any information to external servers.

How Your Data Is Processed

All processing happens entirely on your device:

  • Scanning & OCR — Document images and extracted text never leave your device.
  • Translation — Powered by Google ML Kit running locally. No text is sent to any translation service.
  • Biometric authentication — Face ID and Touch ID are handled by iOS/Android. PaperLens never accesses or stores biometric data.
  • Document storage — All files are stored in your device's local app storage only.
  • Backup — The backup feature creates a local ZIP file that you can save wherever you choose via your device's share sheet. Nothing is uploaded automatically.

Analytics & Tracking

We collect no analytics, crash reports, or usage data. There are no third-party tracking SDKs in PaperLens.

Permissions

  • Camera — To scan documents.
  • Photo Library — To import images from your gallery.
  • Face ID / Touch ID — To lock and unlock secure folders.
  • Contacts — To save business card data you choose to extract.
  • Local Storage — To save scanned documents on your device.

No permission is used beyond its stated purpose.

In-App Purchases

PaperLens Pro purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not handle payment information directly. Purchases are subject to Apple's and Google's respective terms and privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

PaperLens does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms. The version and effective date are shown at the top of this page.
