Why ToolTrunk

Real instruments, not toys

Built around your phone's hardware — accelerometer, magnetometer, microphone, torch. Calibrated, accurate, fast.

Sensor-powered accuracy

Each tool taps the right device sensor — bubble level uses the accelerometer with a low-pass filter, compass uses the magnetometer with tilt compensation, sound meter samples the mic at native rates.

100% on-device

Nothing leaves your phone. No accounts. No network for any tool. Locally stored preferences (theme, calibration) persist until you uninstall.

Designed, not assembled

Premium UI, smooth haptics, calibratable instruments, beautiful dark mode. Built solo with care — not a Swiss-army-knife garbage bag.

The toolkit

Six tools, all on-device

Every tool calibratable, every tool accurate, every tool delightful.

Spirit Level
Surface (2D bubble) and bar (1D) modes. Calibrate to any reference plane. Gentle haptic when you hit true level.
Compass
True heading with cardinal letters, accuracy indicator, and a bearing-lock so you can keep a target while you move.
Sound Meter
Live decibel reading with a half-circle gauge, peak/average stats, and a smooth chart history of the last 90 samples.
Flashlight
Steady torch, SOS pattern, strobe, plus a colored screen-light when your camera flash isn't enough.
Ruler
On-screen ruler with credit-card calibration. Drag the line, see millimetres, centimetres, or inches with sub-mm precision.
Protractor
Touch-based angle measurement with degree ticks along the arc, snap-to-common-angles (30/45/60/90), and supplementary readout.
