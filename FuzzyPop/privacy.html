Welcome to Fuzzy Pop!

PlayPal Apps ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and your children's privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use Fuzzy Pop! ("the App"). We built Fuzzy Pop! for families, and keeping it safe for kids is our top priority.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have questions, please reach out to us at our support page.

Information We Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect

We take a minimal data approach. Fuzzy Pop! does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your location data
  • Photos, contacts, or device files
  • Any information from children under 13
  • Account registration information (no sign-up required!)

Information That May Be Collected Automatically

Our advertising partners (see Advertising section below) may collect limited, non-personal technical data to serve appropriate ads, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • General app usage statistics (which games are played)
  • Crash reports to help us fix bugs

This data is aggregated and anonymised — it cannot be used to identify you personally.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Fuzzy Pop! is designed for all ages, including children under 13. We comply fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international laws. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Our app is rated for general audiences, and all advertising displayed within the app meets child-safe standards. We do not display behavioural or targeted advertising to users we identify as children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has inadvertently provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

Advertising

Fuzzy Pop! is free to play and is supported by advertising. We work with trusted advertising partners who are committed to child-safe advertising standards. All ads displayed are:

  • Screened for appropriateness for family audiences
  • Compliant with COPPA and applicable child-safety regulations
  • Non-targeted when shown to younger users

You can remove all ads with a one-time in-app purchase of $1.99. We think that's a fair deal!

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional ad-removal purchase. Any purchases are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store — we never see or store your payment information. These stores have their own privacy policies which govern your purchase data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share limited, anonymised app usage data with:

  • Analytics providers — to understand how the app is performing
  • Advertising partners — to display appropriate ads (non-personal, aggregated data only)
  • Crash reporting services — to identify and fix bugs

All third-party partners are contractually required to handle data responsibly and in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data associated with the App. Since we collect minimal data, your risk is minimal. The App works fully offline, so most gameplay generates no network traffic at all.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • The right to know what data is held about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of data selling (we don't sell data, so this is always satisfied)
  • For EU/UK residents: rights under GDPR including access, rectification, and erasure
  • For California residents: rights under CCPA

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "last updated" date at the top of this page and, for material changes, notify users through the App. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please visit our Support page. We're a small, friendly team and we'd love to hear from you!
