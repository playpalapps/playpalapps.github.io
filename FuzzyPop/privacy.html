Privacy Policy

We believe in total transparency. Here's exactly what we do (and don't) with your data.

📅 Last Updated: April 17, 2026

The short version: Fuzzy Pop! does not collect any personal information from you. No accounts, no emails, no tracking. We serve child-safe, non-personalised ads only.

1. Who We Are

Fuzzy Pop! is published by PlayPal Apps. You can reach us at playpalapps2024@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. Fuzzy Pop! does not require you to create an account, enter a name, provide an email address, or share any personal details to play.

The only data stored is your game progress and high scores, saved locally on your device using standard platform mechanisms (UserDefaults on iOS, SharedPreferences on Android). This data never leaves your device and is never sent to us.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Fuzzy Pop! is free to play and is supported by advertising via Google AdMob. We have configured AdMob with the following child-safety settings:

  • Child-directed treatment (COPPA): All ad requests are tagged TagForChildDirectedTreatment = True. This disables personalised ads, remarketing and behavioural targeting entirely.
  • Under-age of consent (GDPR): TagForUnderAgeOfConsent = True is set for all requests, disabling consent-based data processing for users in the EEA and UK.
  • Content rating: MaxAdContentRating = G restricts all served ads to General Audience rating only — no adult content can appear.
  • No Advertising ID: The Android Advertising ID permission (AD_ID) has been removed from our app manifest. We do not use the Advertising ID.
  • No ATT: We never call App Tracking Transparency on iOS. No tracking authorisation is requested.

Google AdMob's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

4. In-App Purchases

Fuzzy Pop! offers an optional "Remove Ads" purchase. All payment transactions are handled entirely by Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). We never see, store or process your payment information. Please refer to Apple's or Google's privacy policies for details on how they handle payment data.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

We take children's privacy very seriously. Fuzzy Pop! does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app requires no registration, no login and no personal information to play.

Ads served within the app are non-personalised and restricted to G-rated content only, in full compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

If you believe a child has somehow provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at playpalapps2024@gmail.com.

6. Analytics & Crash Reporting

We do not use any analytics service. We do not track gameplay sessions, screen time or user behaviour. Crash reports may be automatically collected by Apple (via Xcode Organizer) or Google (via Play Console) as part of their standard platform services — we use these only to fix bugs.

7. Data Storage & Servers

PlayPal Apps operates no servers. We do not store any user data in the cloud or any remote database. All game data (scores, preferences) resides solely on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA, UK or another jurisdiction with data protection laws, you have rights including access, rectification, erasure, restriction and data portability. Since we collect and store no personal data about you, these rights are satisfied by design. If you have any questions, contact us at playpalapps2024@gmail.com.

9. Third-Party Links

The app does not contain links to third-party websites. This website may contain links to Google's privacy policy and Apple/Google store pages. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy we will update the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? We're happy to help.
