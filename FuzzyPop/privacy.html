Privacy Policy

Fuzzy Pop! • PlayPal Apps • Effective Date: April 17, 2026

👋 Introduction

Welcome to Fuzzy Pop! ("the App"), a casual mini-game collection published by PlayPal Apps ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the App on iOS and Android devices.

🛡️ Bottom line: We do not collect, store, or sell any personal information about you or your child. The App is designed with privacy first.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at support@playpalapps.com.

📋 Information We Collect

We collect no personal information directly from you. Specifically:

  • We do not ask for or store your name, email address, phone number, or postal address.
  • We do not require account creation or user registration of any kind.
  • We do not collect your location data (GPS, IP-derived, or otherwise).
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, or photo library.
  • We do not track or store gameplay statistics on any server we operate.
  • We have no servers that store user data — there is no PlayPal Apps backend database.

All game progress is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us.

📢 Advertising — Google AdMob

Fuzzy Pop! is free to play and is supported by advertising provided by Google AdMob, a service operated by Google LLC. We have configured AdMob with the following child-safety and privacy protections in place at the SDK level:

✅ Child-Directed (COPPA) ✅ Non-Personalized Only ✅ G-Rated Content ✅ GDPR Compliant ✅ No Ad Tracking ID
  • TagForChildDirectedTreatment = true — All ad requests are flagged as child-directed under the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). This means Google will not use cookies or device identifiers to serve personalized ads.
  • MaxAdContentRating = G — Only G-rated, family-appropriate ad content is permitted. No mature, violent, or inappropriate ad content will be shown.
  • TagForUnderAgeOfConsent = true — Under GDPR, ads are served without user consent dialogs because no personal data is processed for ad targeting.
  • Non-personalized ads only — No behavioral targeting, no interest-based advertising, no remarketing. Ads may be contextually relevant to the game genre but are not tailored to you as an individual.
  • No Advertising ID (IDFA/GAID) used — The app does not request or use Apple's IDFA or Google's Advertising ID for ad targeting.

AdMob may collect certain technical, non-personal information such as device type, OS version, and general geographic region (country level) for fraud prevention and ad delivery purposes. This is governed by Google's own privacy policy, which you can review at: policies.google.com/privacy.

You can permanently remove all ads from the App with a one-time "Remove Ads" in-app purchase for $1.99. Once ads are removed, AdMob is not loaded and no ad-related data collection occurs.

🛒 In-App Purchases

Fuzzy Pop! offers a single optional in-app purchase: "Remove Ads" for a one-time payment of $1.99 USD.

  • All payment processing is handled exclusively by Apple App Store (on iOS) or Google Play (on Android) through their native payment systems.
  • We never see, receive, or store your payment card number, billing address, or any financial information.
  • The Unity IAP SDK is used solely to initiate the platform's native purchase flow — it does not independently collect payment data.
  • Purchase records (e.g., proof of purchase to restore your "Remove Ads" status) are handled entirely by the platform and stored only on your device.

Refund requests must be submitted through Apple or Google, in accordance with their respective refund policies.

👧 Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Fuzzy Pop! is designed for all ages, including young children, and is rated 4+ on the App Store and Everyone on Google Play.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13, in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

  • No personal data is collected from any user, including children under 13.
  • All advertising is configured as child-directed with non-personalized, G-rated content only (see Advertising section above).
  • No social features, chat, or user-generated content are present in the App.
  • No push notifications are sent by the App.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately at support@playpalapps.com and we will take prompt corrective action.

📊 Analytics & Crash Reporting

PlayPal Apps does not use any first-party analytics service. We have no analytics dashboard and do not receive event tracking data, session data, or behavioral data from users.

However, the following platform-level SDKs may collect anonymized crash and performance data as part of their standard operation:

  • Unity Engine — may collect anonymized crash reports and performance metrics per Unity's own privacy policy.
  • Apple (iOS) — may collect crash reports if the user has opted into sharing diagnostics with developers via iOS Settings.
  • Google Play (Android) — may collect Android vitals data (crash rates, ANR rates) for Play Console quality reporting.

None of this data is personally identifiable and we use it only to improve app stability. We have no ability to link crash reports to individual users.

🗄️ Data Retention

PlayPal Apps retains no user data on any server because we operate no data servers. All App data (game progress, settings, purchase status) is stored exclusively on your local device.

Uninstalling the App from your device will remove all locally stored App data. We are unable to recover this data once the App is uninstalled.

🌍 Your Rights — GDPR (EU/EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of Access — You may request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten") — You may request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability — You may request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to Rectification — You may request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Object — You may object to processing of your personal data.

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, delete, or export. Any such request will receive a confirmation that no personal data is held.

To exercise any of these rights, contact: support@playpalapps.com.
