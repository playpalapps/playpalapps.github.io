Gameplay

How do I play Astro Orbit?

Tap anywhere on the screen to jump from your current orbital ring to the next one. Time your jumps carefully to avoid red obstacles. Each ring you land on earns you points. Golden rings award double points!

What are the difficulty levels?

There are three modes: Easy (slower orbits, obstacles appear later), Normal (balanced challenge), and Hard (faster orbits, obstacles appear much earlier). You can change difficulty from the main menu.

How do power-ups work?

Power-ups appear randomly on rings. Shield (blue diamond) absorbs one hit. Magnet (gold star) attracts nearby gems. Slow Time (purple circle) temporarily slows everything down. They activate automatically when collected.

What are near-miss bonuses?

If you land on a ring very close to an obstacle without hitting it, you earn a bonus point and see a "CLOSE!" notification. It rewards precision and risky plays.

Skins & Progression

How do I unlock planet skins?

Planet skins unlock when you reach specific score thresholds. Keep playing and improving your high score to unlock all 10 celestial bodies, from Mercury all the way to the Sun.

What are gems used for?

Gems are the in-game currency. You can earn them by collecting them during gameplay or by watching rewarded video ads. Gems can be used to continue a run after a game over (costs 10 gems).

How do achievements work?

There are 10 achievements to unlock, including your first jump, reaching various score milestones, building combo chains, and achieving near-miss challenges. Check your progress from the main menu.

Technical

The game is running slowly. What can I do?

Try closing other apps running in the background. Make sure your device has sufficient storage space. Restarting the app or your device can also help resolve performance issues.

My progress was lost. Can it be recovered?

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app or clear app data, progress cannot be recovered. We recommend keeping the app installed to preserve your scores and unlocks.

Is an internet connection required?

No. Astro Orbit can be played entirely offline. An internet connection is only needed to view rewarded ads for bonus gems.
