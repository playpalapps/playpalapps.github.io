Privacy

Inkline collects nothing.

We do not collect, transmit, store on a server, share, or sell any data about you. The app runs entirely on your device.

What is saved on your device

Your progress, settings, and revealed forms are saved locally using your phone's secure preferences storage:

  • Which puzzles you've solved.
  • Which sumi-e forms are placed in your Garden.
  • Your audio, haptics, and motion preferences.
  • Whether you've seen the chapter introductions.

Nothing in this list ever leaves your phone. If you delete the app, all of it is deleted with it. There is no cloud backup, no "your account on another device," no recoverable history.

What we don't collect

  • No accounts. No sign-in.
  • No email address. No name. No phone number.
  • No analytics. No usage telemetry. No crash reporting.
  • No ad networks. No advertising IDs.
  • No location. No contacts. No camera or microphone access.
  • No social features. No comments. No multiplayer. No leaderboards.
  • No third-party SDKs collecting on our behalf.

The only network calls Inkline makes

Inkline is single-player and offline-first. The only requests the app makes over the network are to Apple's and Google's in-app purchase services, which the app uses to verify your one-time unlock. Those services are governed by Apple's and Google's privacy policies, not ours.

Inkline does not download new puzzles, new chapters, or new artwork from the network. Everything you see in the app ships with the app.

Children

Inkline is suitable for all ages and is rated for everyone. It contains no chat, no text input, no objectionable content, and no advertising. Because it collects no personal information, no parental consent or age verification is required, but parents and guardians are of course welcome to play it with their children.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, we'll update the version at the bottom of this page and note what changed. Inkline's design intent is to never collect your data — so we expect this policy to stay essentially the same for as long as the app exists.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about privacy in Inkline, write to us at playpalapps2024@gmail.com. We read every email and reply in plain words.
